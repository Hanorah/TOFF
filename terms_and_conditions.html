Terms and Conditions for TOF NATION Membership

  1. Eligibility: To join TOF NATION, you must be a teenager aged between 13 and 19 years old.
  2. Code of Conduct: As a member of TOF NATION, you agree to abide by our code of conduct, which includes respecting others, maintaining a positive attitude, and contributing constructively to the community.
  3. Privacy Policy: Your privacy is important to us. Please review our Privacy Policy to understand how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.
  4. Community Guidelines: TOF NATION has established community guidelines to ensure a safe and inclusive environment for all members. Please familiarize yourself with these guidelines and adhere to them at all times. See below for the guidelines.

Community Guidelines

  • Respect: Treat all members of the community with respect and kindness. Do not engage in any form of harassment, discrimination, or bullying.
  • Positive Attitude: Maintain a positive attitude in your interactions with others. Avoid negative or inflammatory language.
  • Constructive Contribution: Contribute to the community in a constructive and meaningful way. Share helpful resources, participate in discussions, and support fellow members.
  • Safety: Ensure the safety and well-being of yourself and others. Do not share personal information or engage in activities that could compromise security.
  • Inclusivity: Embrace diversity and inclusivity within the community. Be open-minded and respectful of different backgrounds, perspectives, and opinions.
  • Compliance: Follow all applicable laws, regulations, and community guidelines. Do not engage in any illegal or prohibited activities.
  • Reporting: Report any violations of the community guidelines to the appropriate authorities or moderators. Help maintain a safe and welcoming environment for all members.
